## Summary

This feature implements two high-fidelity SpecKit skills: `/roadmap.review` and `/roadmap.optimize`.
- `/roadmap.review` audits the project roadmap (`EPICS.md`) against the mission (`GOALS.md`) and principles (`constitution.md`) to identify functional gaps and alignment issues.
- `/roadmap.optimize` analyzes implicit and explicit dependencies between epics to suggest an efficient execution order that respects both priorities and blocking relationships.
These skills are developed as Markdown-based procedures that leverage the agent's reasoning capabilities to perform structural and logical analysis of project metadata.
