## Summary

This feature implements two new SpecKit skills, `/project.goals` and `/project.epics`, to streamline the initial project setup and roadmap definition. These skills are designed as Markdown-based natural language procedures that guide an AI agent to produce structured `GOALS.md` and `EPICS.md` files. The `/project.epics` skill specifically ensures that the generated roadmap adheres to the strict schema required by the `speckit-loop` autonomous agent, maintaining alignment with the project's `constitution.md`.
